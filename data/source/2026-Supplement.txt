ABOUT COASTER
OUR BACKGROUND 
Coaster Fine Furniture  was founded 
in 1981 with a simple goal in mind: to 
exceed customer expectations in the 
home furnishing industry. From the very 
beginning, we have been committed 
to providing innovative products, 
exceptional value, and responsive 
customer service. As an importer 
and distributor of fine furniture, we 
have grown into a leading brand that 
is recognized for our commitment to 
excellence.
OPERATION  
AND SERVICE
As a leading North American distributor, 
Coaster has 6 distribution centers 
and over 400 employees. Our culture 
of respect and cooperation creates 
an efficient brand experience for 
consumers and positive growth for 
dealers. We offer diverse and stylish 
furniture at a competitive price point 
without sacrificing quality or service. 
With responsive service and rapid 
delivery, we support our customers 
throughout the US and Canada. From 
budget to luxury pieces, our products 
meet a wide range of needs. We take 
pride in offering stylish, functional 
furniture and are dedicated to customer 
satisfaction from order to delivery.
